Grilled Chicken Salad!

Description:

This quick, easy lunch is packed with greens, healthy fats, and protien!

Ingredients are as follows:

  • Chicken Breast
  • Avocado
  • Leafy Greens of your choice

Preparation Steps:

  1. Cook One 1lb. Boneless Chicken Breast
  2. Plate your vegetables
  3. Lay the Chicken on top
  4. EAT IT
