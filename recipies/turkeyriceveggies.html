Ground Turkey, Rice, & Veggies!

Description:

Healthy, quick meal option, you can take with you!

Ingredients:

  • Ground Turkey
  • Grilled Broccoli
  • Brown Rice
  • Optional: BBQ sauce & Cheese

Preparation Steps:

  1. Steam Brown Rice.
  2. Roast veggies on 350 for 15 minutes.
  3. Warm the ground turkey in the microwave on High for 3 minutes. Stir and let cool.
  4. Combine however you see fit, and enjoy!
